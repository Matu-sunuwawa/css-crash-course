welcome

Detectives Shorty Sherlock and Wallaby Watson look for clues in the Gospel of John to discover “Who is Jesus?” Children are invited to use their sleuthing skills to consider the evidence of the expert witnesses and to respond to what is revealed about Jesus in the Gospel of John. This creative curriculum is designed to reach out to children anywhere, anytime and uses dramas and small group time to engage children. Step-by-step guidance is provided for a 10-day curriculum. Includes a guide for leaders, materials for creative Bible exploration, daily drama scripts, Bible Diggers Small Group Activities
